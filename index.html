Booking process

How To Book ANANT SHREE Residential Homes

1

Fill Enquiry Form

Submit your basic details through the official project form to receive a callback and schedule your one-on-one discussion with the sales team.

2

Schedule Site Visit

Book a guided site visit to explore sample flats, understand the layout, and experience the on-ground setting around the project location in Nallasopara West.

3

Choose Your Unit

Select your preferred configuration, floor and view. Our staff will help you choose, considering price and availability and your family's unique needs.

4

Complete KYC Formalities

Provide required identification and income proofs (PAN, Aadhar, etc.) to complete buyer authentication and complete project paperwork and home loan eligibility checks.

5

Token Booking & Payment

Pay the token amount to block your unit. You'll receive an official receipt and payment schedule along with further financial consultation if needed.

6

Get Allotment Letter

After verification and payment, receive the official allotment letter with unit details, pricing breakdown and possession timeline documentation from the builder.
